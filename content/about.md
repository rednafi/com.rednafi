Hey there! I'm Redowan Delowar - also go by the handle "rednafi" on the web.

I dabble in platform engineering, which mostly means working with giant distributed balls of
mud and fighting to keep the house of cards from falling over. Lately, I've been focused on
persistence, resilience, and observability - or whatever else is currently on fire. When not
at the keyboard, I'm probably running or reading sci-fi.

Talking points: Go, Python, persistence, consistency, resilience, HA, and sci-fi.

## Colophon

[Hugo] powers this blog. It's rocking the [Papermod] theme with some handrolled CSS flair. I
write in plain Markdown and push the [content] to GitHub, triggering the [GitHub Actions]
CI, which then deploys the site to [GitHub Pages].

More on the [stack, deployment, and writing process] if you're curious.

## Breadcrumbs

- The handle _"rednafi"_ is just a portmanteau of my first name, _Redowan_, and my nickname
  _Nafi_, which my parents and a few close friends still use.
- I like sci-fis, and my all-time favourite book is _[Stanislaw Lem's Solaris]_.
- I think through writing and not the other way around. Most of the blogs on this site take
  way longer to write than I'd like to admit.
